Your Platform to Participate into Writing Contest, Get Published and Share Visuals

Precisely it's an opportunity to take part in different academic writing contest and win cash prizes with certificates and see your creative works in print

  • We conduct creative, factual, persuasive and critical writing contest every month
  • These contest are held according to the observational calendar of United Nations
  • The best one on each category of the contest get published in the ezine first
  • Participants receives cheque and certificate of participation along with feed back
  • All contests are absolutely free; however one has to be a subscribed member to participate
  • Students may also send a short video content on the write up that he/she has contributed
  • Our panel of judges evaluate and award award point on both writing and video presentation
  • Momentum reserves the right to publish those video contents on youtube platform
  • The topic/theme of the writing contest is on the basis of English Curriculam of CBSE, IB & IGCSE

Momentum also organises public speaking events such as deabte, elocution, group discussion, paper reading contest, quiz, dramatics performance, spelling bee, mime, mono acting, recitation and different exhibition of paintings, science models or entrepreneurship programs
